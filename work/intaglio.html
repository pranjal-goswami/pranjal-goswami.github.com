Intaglio - Smart Quiz - IIM Calcutta


Intaglio is a management fest organized by Indian Institute of Management, Calcutta, India. The task was to develop a system capable of generating a number of online quizes where the questions and time limits were obtained from the administrator and to store the user responses with other information viz. time on each question and number of hints used.
